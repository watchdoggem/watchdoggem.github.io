What It Means

How the desk thinks

EMA trend

If price is above the fast and slow EMA stack, trend-following longs get more room to work.

RSI

RSI above 60 supports continuation. RSI below 45 warns that momentum is weak.

MACD

Bullish MACD suggests momentum is expanding. Bearish MACD means strength is fading or leaning lower.

Fib zone

The desk uses the recent swing to mark the classic reaction zone so you do not have to place fibs manually.
