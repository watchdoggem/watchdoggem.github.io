How To Read It

What the snapshot usually means

Price above EMA

Trend has a tailwind. Favor pullbacks, not panic chasing.

RSI above 60

Momentum supports continuation. Good sign, but it can still be too extended to buy immediately.

MACD bullish

Momentum is accelerating. Better when it happens after reclaiming a level, not into resistance.

Fib 0.382 to 0.618

That is the classic pullback zone. Strong trends often react there before continuing.
